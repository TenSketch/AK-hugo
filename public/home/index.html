About AK Foundation

At AK Foundation, we prioritize **அன்னதானம் (Annadhanam)**—providing food to those in need with compassion and care. This initiative is a cornerstone of our commitment to humanity.

We also empower rural youth through the **AK School of Sports Academy**, identifying talent and helping them compete on global stages. Inspired by Shri K. Anantha Krishnan’s vision, we bridge gaps and create opportunities for a brighter future.

Learn More

அன்னதானம்: A Noble Service

Annadhanam is the act of offering food, the highest form of charity. It not only fills stomachs but also touches hearts. "Sharing food is sharing love." Through Annadhanam, we strive to feed the needy and bring communities together, fostering compassion and humanity.
